I identified a row of heavy-duty Fixed Steel Bollards installed along the perimeter of a high-traffic pedestrian mall and storefront area.
These are designed to manage vehicle traffic and prevent unauthorized vehicles from entering pedestrian zones or impacting building storefronts.

Control Type: This is a Physical Preventative Control.

CIA Triad - Availability: In cybersecurity, "Availability" ensures that systems and data are accessible when needed. Bollards protect the Availability of the businesses and their IT infrastructure (like Point-of-Sale systems and local servers) by preventing "smash-and-grab" or vehicle-ramming attacks that would cause catastrophic hardware destruction and long-term service outages.
